How to make the perfect popcorn

* To make the perfect popcorn, you will need the following ingredients:

* 1/2 cup Corn Pop Corn kernels
* 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
* 1/2 teaspoon of salt

* Instructions:

1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add the corn kernels and salt to the pan.
3. Cover the pan with a lid and cook until the popping slows down to one pop every second.
4. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
