A Little History:

Popcorn corn, also known as "popped corn" or "popcorn", has an ancient history.
It is believed that its origin dates back to the indigenous people of Central and South America, who already cultivated it and consumed for thousands of years.
Over time, popcorn spread throughout the world, conquering palates and becoming a popular snack in diverse cultures.

The Secret of the Explosion:

What makes popcorn so special is its unique ability to transform.
When heated, the water present inside the grain expands, breaking the hard shell and releasing the starch, which turns into a soft white mass.
This process, known as "popping", is what gives rise to the popcorn we love so much.
