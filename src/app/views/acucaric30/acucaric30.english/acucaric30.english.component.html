ICUMSA 30 sugar



Refers to a specific type of white sugar with an Icumsa value of 30. This means that it is very white and has few impurities. It is often used in industrial applications and in the manufacture of food products that require high-quality sugar.

Icumsa 30 sugar is one of the most refined and purest types of sugar available on the market. Its high quality and whiteness make it a popular choice in various industrial applications and the food industry. Here are some important points about Icumsa 30 sugar:

Refining Process: Icumsa 30 sugar goes through a rigorous refining process. During this process, impurities, dyes and other unwanted compounds are removed, resulting in extremely white and crystalline sugar.

International Standard: The term “Icumsa” stands for “International Commission for Uniform Methods of Sugar Analysis”. The Icumsa value is a measure of the color of sugar, based on the amount of impurities present. The lower the Icumsa value, the whiter and purer the sugar.

Applications: Icumsa 30 sugar is used in various applications including:

Food Industry: It is used in the manufacture of sweets, cakes, biscuits and other confectionery products.
Beverages: It is added to drinks such as iced teas, juices and soft drinks.
Pharmaceutical Industry: Can be used as an excipient in medicines.
Chemical Industry: It is used in chemical processes and fermentations.

Guaranteed Quality: Icumsa 30 sugar meets international quality and purity standards. Its low concentration of impurities makes it ideal for use in products that require a high standard of quality.

In summary, Icumsa 30 sugar is a reliable choice for those looking for a high-quality, white and pure product. Its versatility and applicability make it essential in various industries.
