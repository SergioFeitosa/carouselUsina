ICUMSA 100-150 sugar



ICUMSA 100-150 sugar is an intermediate type within the ICUMSA scale, falling between ICUMSA 45 and ICUMSA VHP. Let's explore more about it:

What is ICUMSA 100-150 sugar?

ICUMSA 100-150 represents an intermediate level of purity.
Its polarization is at least 99.50%.
It has a moderate sucrose content, making it suitable for various industrial applications.
Its color varies from light to medium brown.
The particle size is in the range of 0.9 to 1.2 mm.

Applications:

ICUMSA 100-150 is widely used in food processing and certain industrial applications. It offers a balance between purity and economy, being suitable for situations that do not require the absolute whiteness of ICUMSA 45.

Therefore, if you are looking for a sugar with intermediate characteristics, ICUMSA 100-150 sugar may be a suitable choice for your needs!
