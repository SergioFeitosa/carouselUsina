Non-GMO Corn: A Conscious and Healthy Choice

Non-GMO corn (Genetically Modified Organisms) stands out as a healthy and sustainable alternative for consumers looking for more natural foods that are free from genetic engineering.
Grown without the use of genetic modification techniques, non-GMO corn offers several benefits for the health and for the environment.

A More Natural Choice:

GMO-free: Produced without the use of genetic modification techniques, ensuring that the plant's DNA original be preserved.
No artificial additives: Does not contain artificial or synthetic ingredients, such as pesticides and fertilizers chemicals, which can be harmful to health and the environment.
Authentic flavor and aroma: Preserves the natural flavor and aroma of corn, providing a unique experience gastronomic more authentic and tasty.

Health Benefits:

More nutritious: Studies indicate that non-GMO corn may have a higher content of essential nutrients, such as vitamins, minerals and antioxidants, compared to GMO corn.
Less allergenic:The genetic modification process can increase the allergenic potential of some foods. O Non-GMO corn is a safer option for people with food allergies.
Promotes biodiversity: The production of non-GMO corn contributes to the preservation of biodiversity, protecting the traditional corn varieties and environmental balance.

Environmental Sustainability:

Fewer pesticides: Growing non-GMO corn generally uses fewer pesticides, reducing the ground contamination and water, and protecting the health of farmers and consumers.
Greater soil fertility: Sustainable agricultural practices, such as crop rotation and green manure, used in the Non-GMO corn cultivation contributes to soil preservation and fertility.
Combating climate change: The production of non-GMO corn emits fewer greenhouse gases, contributing to the mitigating climate change and for a more sustainable future.

A Promising Future:

Demand for non-GMO foods is growing around the world, driven by consumer awareness about benefits to health and the environment.
Brazil, one of the largest corn producers in the world, has great potential to become a global leader in production of non-GMO corn, meeting growing market demand and contributing to a brighter future sustainable and healthy.
By choosing non-GMO corn, you are making a conscious and responsible choice for your health, the environment and for the future of the planet.
