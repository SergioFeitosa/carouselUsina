ICUMSA 45 sugar



ICUMSA 45 sugar is one of the most sought after types by global sugar importers due to its purity and degree of whiteness. Let's explore more about it:

What is ICUMSA 45 sugar?

The term “ICUMSA” stands for “International Commission for Uniform Methods of Sugar Analysis”. It is an acronym that denotes quality standards for different types of sugar.

ICUMSA 45 sugar is known as white sugar. It is highly refined and suitable for human consumption and use in a wide range of food production. Its appearance is sparkling white and its purity is high, with 99.8% sucrose in its composition. The ICUMSA 45 sugar refinement process removes bacteria and contaminants present in raw sugar, making it safer for consumption.

Characteristics of ICUMSA 45 Sugar:

Polarization: Minimum 99.80 degrees.
Ash content: Maximum 0.04%.
Solubility: 100% (dry and free from retardation).
Color: Sparkling white.
Granulation: Fine.
Moisture: Maximum 0.04%.


Applications:

ICUMSA 45 sugar is commonly used in the food and beverage industry, especially in applications where a pure white appearance is crucial. Its solubility is 100% DTR (Within Internationally Accepted Limits) and free flowing, ensuring ease of application. Therefore, if you are looking for high quality sugar for your needs, ICUMSA 45 sugar is an excellent choice!
